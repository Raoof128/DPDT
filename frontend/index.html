üìñ Spectral Signatures

Spectral analysis uses SVD/PCA to detect poisoned samples by identifying outliers in the spectral domain. Backdoor triggers often create separable subspaces in the representation space.

How it works:

  1. Compute feature embeddings for all samples
  2. Apply SVD to get singular vectors
  3. Identify samples with high projections onto top singular vectors
  4. Flag outliers as potentially poisoned
